It's spring and asparagus are in season! A great way to enjoy them is sauteed with some shallots and cooked up with eggs into an easy frittata.

    Ingredients:
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup sliced shallots
  • 1 pound thin spear asparagus, tough ends snapped off, spears cut diagonally into 1-inch lengths
  • 6 large eggs
  • 3/4 cup ricotta cheese (can sub cottage chz)
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon minced fresh chives
  • 1/4 teaspoon dried tarragon
  • 1 cup shredded Gruyere or Swiss cheese (can sub an italian blend)

    Directions:
  1. Step 1:
    Cook shallots in butter:
    Head butter into a 10-inch oven-proof frying pan over medium heat.
    Add shallots and cook, stirring occasionally, until they often and turn translucent, about 3 minutes.
  2. Step 2:
    Add asparagus:
    Cook for an additional 3 minutes.
  3. Add egg mixture:
    Beat the eggs and ricotta cheese together, add salt, stir in the chives and tarragon.
  4. Top with cheese and broil:
    Sprinkle gruyere cheese over the eggs and pout in oven to broil until cheese is melted and browned, and the center is set, about 6 to 8 minutes.
