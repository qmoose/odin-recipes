This recipe for Brenda's lasagna incorporates lean ground beef, garlic, two shredded cheeses, and an entire pint of ricotta cheese. Yum!

Ingredients:

  • 1 16 ounce package lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 16 ounce jar of spaghetti sauce
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/2 pound shredded mozzarella
  • 1/2 pound shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 pint ricotta cheese
  1. Step 1:
    Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
  2. Step 2:
    Prehead oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). In a large skillet over medium-high heat, brown beef and season with salt and pepper; drain. Stir in spaghetti sauce and garlica nd simmer 5 minutes.
  3. Step 3:
    In a medium bowl, combine mozzarella, cheddar, and ricotta; stir well. In a 9x13 inch pan, alternate layers of noodles, meat mixture, and cheese mixture until pan is filled.
  4. Step 4:
    Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes, or until cheese is melted and bubbly.
